Welcome to my website! My name is Keyhan Gavahi, and I am passionate about machine learning, statistics, satellite remote sensing, parallel computing, and hydrology. With a PhD in computational hydrology (GPA 4.0) and a master's degree in computer science (GPA 4.0) from the University of Alabama, I possess a strong academic background and expertise in these fields.

Throughout my academic journey, I have focused on the intersection of machine learning, statistics, remote sensing, parallel computing, and hydrology. My interdisciplinary approach has allowed me to explore innovative solutions to complex problems.

I have +7 years of experience in Python programming and machine learning, and analyzed >100 TB of geospatial data. I am well-versed in designing and implementing efficient algorithms and models. Drawing upon my Master's degree in Computer Science and complemented by specialized knowledge in Statistics, I have expanded my expertise in AI and ML frameworks. This augmented skill set empowers me as a data scientist to create data-driven solutions for real-world business problems.
